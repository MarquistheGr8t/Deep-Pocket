Charting Language
×
💎
Diamond (Sustain)
Hit together and let it ring. Band stops the groove, drums sustain the cymbal — no time, just the held moment.
K-S-K/K
Kick-Snare-Double Kick
Kick · Snare · Kick-Kick. The slash means the last two kicks are tied tight — quick 16th notes back-to-back. Drives the chorus pulse.
K/K
Double Kick
Two quick kick drum hits tied together — fast 16th notes back-to-back.
k on 4s
Kick on 4s
Kick drum on every beat (1, 2, 3, 4). Steady four-on-the-floor pulse.
low
Low / Soft
Pull back — minimal volume, light touch. Often hi-hat only or ride with no kick.
→
Transition / Into
Leads into the next thing. "low → all in" means start low, then build to full band.
build
Build
Steadily grow energy — usually toms + snare escalating into a chorus or peak moment.
*accent
Accent Marker
A hit tied to a specific lyric or band cue. "*angels cry" means the hit lands on that lyric.
:break
Break
Full band cuts out briefly before coming back in.
:swell
Swell
Cymbal swell or roll growing in volume — for endings or builds.
Tag
Tag / Repeat Moment
A short repeated phrase — usually after a chorus, often with a 💎 or fill.
Turn
Turnaround
Short transition connecting two parts — usually 2 or 4 bars.
